Introduction

The platform can be used to create custom, personalized functionality to support every department at your company, from HR to Finance to IT. When you’re thinking about processes to build on the platform, identify use cases with the highest impact and lowest effort first.

Here are some tips for identifying good processes to bring onto the platform. Look for processes with:

  • Reliance on spreadsheets
  • Collaboration via email
  • Documents shared on local file directories
  • Time-intensive, manual steps
  • Impact to a few departments maximum (just as you’re getting started; keeping your number of stakeholders to a minimum will help you make progress)

Let’s take a look at a few specific use cases in detail.

How HR Can Use the Platform

Speed up the onboarding process with a custom onboarding app. Using the platform, create a series of objects and automated processes to manage all of the steps needed to onboard a new employee. This could include:

  • New hire orientation
  • Training plans
  • Equipment orders
  • Software and hardware installation and setup
  • Automated tasks and reminders for the employee, manager, and hiring manager
  • Time off tracking

The onboarding app can also be made available for mobile, so all team members can access the app from the Salesforce1 Mobile App.

How IT Can Use the Platform

Simplify the IT help desk ticketing process by building on the same platform as your CRM. Use custom objects, or customize cases to manage internal service requests from employees. Assign work to queues and use Chatter to collaborate on requests, and track everything in Salesforce. This could include:

  • Reports and dashboards to view requests at an aggregate level.
  • Confirmation email automation when requests are received, completed, or more information is needed.
  • Queueing of incoming requests.
  • Resubmission of employee requests via a form, or through a self-service community, with a knowledge base for common issues

Through using the platform, IT can use a combination of standard and custom functionality to support the IT help desk process.
